Realm of Ways

Since the dawn of time, there has been a hidden power, a force waiting to be discovered. It was always just a matter of time until the "Portals" were found. Meet Bob - The Protagonist

On an abandoned planet, Bob is the only human, surrounded by animals and plants that can speak, spending his days exploring every corner of his world, uncovering secrets and making friends in the most unexpected places. His next big adventure is just around the corner!
